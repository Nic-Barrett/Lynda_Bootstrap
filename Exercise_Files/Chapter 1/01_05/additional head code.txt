Respond.js script available at:

https://github.com/scottjehl/Respond


Download respond.min.js, place in js folder, link in the HEAD of your HTML document
	<script src="js/respond.js"></script>

In the body of your document, just above </body>, place these links:

	<script src="http://code.jquery.com/jquery-latest.min.js"></script>
    	<script src="js/bootstrap.min.js"></script>
